Gradient Thoughts

Gradient: an increase or decrease in the magnitude of a property observed in passing from one point or moment to another.

It's really cool how our thoughts change with time, there are generally 3 factors resulting in that change of thoughts: life experiences, genetics, and education; These factors have a pretty good gradience, and so my thoughts change quite often like everyone else, so in this page I'll share some of my passing thoughts so that at a given point I can compare the thoughts I had and the ones I have now.
